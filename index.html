Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Realive is built with a simple principle: we only access what the app needs to function, and we never store or sell your personal data. This policy explains exactly what we do and don't collect.

01

Data Collection

Realive does not collect, store, or transmit any unnecessary personal data. We only process information required for the App's core functionality.

We do not maintain user accounts, we do not track usage analytics, and we do not use third-party advertising SDKs. Your use of the App remains private to you.

No account required. No analytics tracking. No advertising identifiers collected.

02

Camera and Microphone Access

The App requires access to your device's camera and microphone to broadcast video and audio. This is the core purpose of the application — without these permissions, the App cannot function.

All media captured by your camera and microphone is transmitted directly to your selected streaming platform (such as Twitch, YouTube, Facebook, or others) in real time. This media is not stored on our servers at any point.

Your stream goes directly from your device to your chosen platform. We are not an intermediary that stores or processes your video or audio.

03

Third-Party Services

By streaming to third-party platforms through the App, you are subject to their respective privacy policies. Each platform — Twitch, YouTube, Facebook, TikTok, Kick, and others — handles your data according to their own terms.

We do not control how these platforms collect, store, or use your data, your stream content, or information about your viewers. We strongly encourage you to review the privacy policy of any platform you choose to stream to.

04

Local Storage

The App stores certain preferences locally on your device — such as your selected language, theme, stream key (encrypted), and stream settings. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

You can clear this data at any time by uninstalling the App.

05

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the App or applicable law. When we do, we will update the date at the top of this page.

Your continued use of the App after any changes to this policy will indicate your acceptance of the updated terms. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

06

Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how the App handles data, please reach out to us directly.
